Math Resources

MATLAB provides an environment in which programming and mathematics skills combine. It is often used in various STEM curricula and may be available for free through universities (CU Denver students: click here to download).

MathWorks offers a few free, interactive tutorials. The MATLAB Onramp course is a great introduction to the available tools, and enough to get started with physics calculations.

WolframAlpha can be used for calculations and provides a breakdown of the mathematics involved. Click here for the WolframAlpha Physics Example page.
